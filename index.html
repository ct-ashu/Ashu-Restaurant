About Ashu Restaurant

Our Story

Ashu Restaurant is a family-owned restaurant that has been serving the Nawada community for over 10 years. Our passion for food and hospitality drives us to create a memorable dining experience for our guests.

We take pride in using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to prepare our dishes, ensuring that every meal is not only delicious but also supports our local farmers.
